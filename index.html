Welcome to Zoo Cosmos

Welcome to Zoo Cosmos, a vibrant sanctuary nestled in the heart of Navi Dharangaon, India. Here, you can embark on an unforgettable journey through nature, exploring a diverse collection of animals and birds in thoughtfully designed habitats that reflect their natural environments. Whether you’re a family, wildlife enthusiast, or student eager to learn, our park offers engaging exhibits and educational programs for all ages. We invite you to connect with wildlife, discover the wonders of nature, and participate in our conservation efforts to protect these magnificent creatures for generations to come. Enjoy your visit!
